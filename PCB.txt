#5V Transistor Relay Driver Circuit



#Symbols:

Q1 – NPN Transistor

R – Resistor

D1 – Diode

LED – Indicator

RLY – Relay



#Specifications:

Supply: 5V

Transistor: 2N3904

Relay: 5V (SRD-05VDC-SL-C)

Diode: 1N4007 (Flyback Protection)



#Importance:

Used to control high-power loads using a low-power signal in electronic and automation circuits.
